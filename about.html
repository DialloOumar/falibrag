About Falibra Group

Building excellence through experience, innovation, and commitment

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Falilou Diallo, Falibra Group has rapidly emerged as Guinea's leading construction and digitalization firm. Over the past 7 years, we have built a reputation for excellence, innovation, and reliability in delivering complex infrastructure projects across West Africa, specializing in construction and biometric solutions.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to transform Guinea's construction landscape through quality craftsmanship and innovative solutions. Today, we stand as a testament to what vision, dedication, and strategic partnerships can achieve.
